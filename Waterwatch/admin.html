Role Definitions
Citizen Community Member

Any verified South African resident. Can view outage reports, water quality data, and municipal updates. Can report outages and subscribe to alerts for their area.

Municipal Municipal Official

Verified municipal staff. Can publish official updates, manage outage tickets for their municipality, view operational reports, and broadcast alerts. Requires admin approval on registration.

Admin Platform Administrator

Full platform access. Can manage all users, assign/revoke roles, approve municipal accounts, view audit logs, and configure platform settings. Assigned only by other admins.
